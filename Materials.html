    • Materials

    For this, the items you will need are a bowl and a spoon. The ingredients needed are cereal and milk (or water/orange juice if those are your preferance). If you happen to be alergic to milk or lactose intolerant, use a non-milk milk.

    There are a very large catalog of cereals to choose from, so do some experimentation or stick to a childhood classic. Be sure to read the labels to know exactly what's in the box!

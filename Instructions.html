Instructions

First, place your bowl firmly on a table/counter. Then, open your cereal package (here are some sugar-free options) and pour an optimal amount into the bowl. Then, pour milk into the bowl. Lastly, use your spoon to enjoy your morning meal! As you do, take a moment to mourn (Ha! Mourn-ing meal!) those pour souls that had to consume dried dough prior to the invention of actually good cereal.

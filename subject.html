Making Cereal

    • Materials

    For this, the items you will need are a bowl and a spoon. The ingredients needed are cereal and milk. If you happen to be alergic to milk or lactose intolerant, use a non-milk milk.

    Instructions

    First, place your bowl firmly on a table/counter. Then, open your cereal package (here are some sugar-free options) and pour an optimal amount into the bowl. Then, pour milk into the bowl. Lastly, use your spoon to enjoy your bowl of cereal!

    Tips

    You can also warm the milk if your teeth are sensitive to cold milk. If you prefer pouring the milk first, that works too! Some people like to argue about cereal vs milk first, here's an article about that.
